Best platforms for learning programming

Youtube

Youtube is the free and open source platform to learn and find anything or any information. Millions of people upload various videos on youtube on a regular basis about various topics. Learning programming is quite easy from youtube videos since lot of people upload videos on similar topics, and hey its totally free to use, no subscription, no fees, nothing.

Coursera

Coursera is a great platform to learn various topics and subjects provided by the best colleges and companies. Many universities and companies upload the online courses that they provide. Some of these courses are free where as some need to be paid for, the advantage of coursera is that you are awarded a verified certificate for every course you complete, which can prove very useful. Coursera has large amount of courses on programming and coding made available by lots of notable institutions such as University of California, University of London, John Hopkins univerity, Oracle, IBM,etc.

Udemy

This is a new and fastly growing platform for online learning. Here courses are provided by many people as well as institutions on various topics. This platform has many features revolving around the course to help you learn the skills even better.This too provides a certification after successfully completing a course. But unlike Youtube Udemy has paid courses.Udemy too has a large amount of courses for learning programming.
